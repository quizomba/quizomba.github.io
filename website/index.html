Values Correlation Tool

Trying to help you visualize the intersection between homosexuality acceptance and women's rights.

This is my attempt to create a visualization to investigate a corelation between women's rights and how homosexuality is accept. In order to do that, I crossed 15 variables, both from World Value Survey and Gapminder.

#Visualization Features#
Brushing: it is possible to brush selections.
Reordering: by clicking in the variable name, it is possible to reorder the axis.
Inverting: double-clicking the variable name will reorder the axis.
Responsive Grid: when brushing, the table will update according to selection.

#Good to Know#
Every data has been normalized, in order to fit the scale best. That's why some numbers are negative. The data is incomplete. When a country lacks data for a variable, it is 0. Some waves/time periods do not contain some variables. In those cases, the variable has been deleted.
    Key to Variables:

  • V45 When jobs are scarce, men have a priority.
    Number of people who agree/number of people who disagree. Bigger numbers mean men have a higher priority.

  • v47 There is a problem in women earning more than men
    Number of people who agree/number of people who disagree. Bigger numbers mean women with higher salaries can be a problem.

  • V51 Men make better decision than women politically
    Number of people who agree/number of people who disagree. Bigger numbers mean men make better decisions.

  • V53 Men make better business executive than woman
    Number of people who agree/number of people who disagree. Bigger numbers mean men make better executives.

  • V139 Democracy: women have the same right as men
    % of people who said women participation is crucial for a country democracy.

  • V48 Having a job is the best way for a woman to be independent
    Number of people who agree/number of people who disagree. Bigger numbers mean having job is more relevant for a woman.

  • V40 Would not like to have as neighbors homosexuals
    Number of people who mentioned homosexuals are not good neighbors/number of people who did not mentioned. Bigger numbers mean homosexuality is more accepted.
